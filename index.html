Cold Smoke

Music band based in Switzerland.
The name "Cold Smoke" refers here to a greatly coveted state of water, where beauty meets emotion : the glittering cloud of powder experienced by free riders when carving lines through a fresh layer of snow. Our music aims to evoke that kind of sensation.
